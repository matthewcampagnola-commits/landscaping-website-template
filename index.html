Our Story

Rooted in Suffolk County for over a decade

MM Landscaping was founded with a simple belief: every home deserves a landscape that reflects its owners' vision. We're a locally owned and operated company, and we take pride in every project we touch — big or small.

Our team of certified landscape professionals brings creativity, horticultural expertise, and genuine care to each property. We work with Long Island's unique soil, climate, and environment to create landscapes that thrive year after year.
